About Me

Peter Kenichi.

Since earning my BA in Economics from the University at Albany in 2022, my professional journey has been marked by a commitment to exploration and growth. While rooted in economics, my career trajectory has led me to diverse experiences. I found myself drawn to the dynamic realm of graphic design, where creativity knows no bounds. Joining a small graphic design company allowed me to immerse myself in this vibrant field, honing my design skills and cultivating a passion for visual storytelling.

Within a remarkably short time frame, I ascended to the role of Operations Manager, overseeing three departments and spearheading efficiency and innovation within the organization. This role has provided me with invaluable opportunities to integrate my analytical acumen with creative problem-solving. Concurrently, I've been deeply engaged in mastering coding languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enhancing my capacity to leverage technology in enhancing operational effectiveness.

Beyond the confines of my professional endeavors, I am a dedicated student, currently preparing for the GMAT as I pursue my MBA. I hold a firm belief in the ethos of lifelong learning, constantly seeking avenues to expand my knowledge and skill set. The world offers boundless opportunities for growth and discovery, and I am fervently committed to exploring these possibilities. When not immersed in work or study, I enjoy exploring the great outdoors, capturing moments through photography, and delving into literature. This website stands as a testament to my journey, showcasing my multifaceted skills, passions, and aspirations. Thank you for joining me on this journey of exploration and growth.
