Ocean Blue Prime

Ocean Blue Prime is a premium steakhouse. Recently established, the client requested menus, outdoor signage, business cards, etc. that fit the vision of their business.

Using the provided logo and color palette I created a modern yet luxurious taste that reflected the products and their prices.

Outdoor signage adorned outside the restaurant to give it a presence in the area while also engaging and bringing in new customers.

Business cards and gift certificates were created to further market their new business within the area.

The design's elegant style allows for the aura that the business was looking to embody.

A&G TRANSPORTATION

A&G Transportation is an upcoming logistics company. This company provides secure carrier shipments and maintains a reputation of being a strong and swift entity to ensure trust between their clients and the company.

Given a logo, the client requested several items that would further market the company as well as provide security.

The trifold as well as bsuiness cards are a means of marketing to future clients, providing them with everything necessary to contact and utilize the business to their advantage.

Identification cards on the other hand are meant for the companys epmloyees, providing a layer of security to their facilities and shipments.

In utilizing all of these components, the company ensures clients that their business is safe in the hands of this quality company.

NBPA

The National Basketball Players Association - a derivative of the NBA, is an union organization of players.

The client requested several items that would be used in gift bags for events that they would be holding throughout the season. The client had also requested practice jerseys with sponsored branding.

Provided the logos, my team and I had to print artwork and produce over 100 drawstring bags, jerseys, as well as other items in time for these events.

Managing a team of five people, this job was turned around in 1.5 weeks.

Santi de Dios

Santi de Dios is a new clothing brand emerging in the streetwear niche.

As they have not yet launched, the client requested a multitude of content to be posted on their social media, as well as mockup designs of their pieces.

As the sole designer of Santi de Dios, my artwork comes from initial images I have taken IRL, then processed with the use of design platforms to create a new style of printwear.

Klean Bee

Klean Bee - a small houskeeping company was searching for a simple and cost efficient means to market their company in order to get new clients.

The client requested a logo, business cards and door hangers that would be handed out and placed at each location they maintained.

The design for this company was based off of the client wanting to follow their business model, quick cleaning by a warm and happy team.

Vermont Good Wood

Vermont Good Wood - lumber and firewood company.

For VGW our project was to wrap a fleet of trucks with the company's branding.

I worked directly with the owners of the company to ensure every detail was correct.

This project entailed mocking up the design on every type of truck the company had, being sure the sizing of the prints were correct in size, and overseeing installation of the graphics onto the truck.

Each truck was completed within a 2 week period. This includes redesign, scaling, printing, and installation.

Curated Colors

Curated Colors - an art curation startup, aims to shed light on the smaller artists of today's world.

Project: Social media posts, apparel, decals, engraved brand tags.

As one of Curated Colors' designers, the image we reflect is extremely important. Exposure and openness are key when it comes to sucess in the art space. This is the vision we enjoy curating.

This project revolved around trying to further the brand's marketing. We created a unique clothing tag for each item purchased. These were made of acrylic or wood in a variety of colors. The stickers were requested and were used as an expression of gratitude with every purchase. Finally, we created engaging posts which led more people to follow and believe in a platform for artists made by artists.

Freelance Apparel

Curated Colors X Santi de Dios

I have made graphic designs and artwork for both of these small clothing brands. Both these brands requested test samples of embroidered and printed apparel. This included but was not limited to hoodies, sweaters, beanies, and t-shirts. Once the artwork was approved, I then created a mockup so the client would be able to approve placement and color codes.

Once proofs have been approved I would scale the designs to the requested size, set up files for printing or embroidery, and finally print or stitch the artwork onto the pieces.
