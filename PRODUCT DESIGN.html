Portfolio - Website

I designed and developed my personal website, peterkenichi.com, to showcase my skills in design and development.

The process began with creating the layout and theme in Figma, followed by wireframing additional pages to refine the structure. I then built the site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, ensuring a responsive and engaging user experience.

After launching, I conducted user testing with a sample group to gather feedback on usability and areas for improvement, refining the site based on their insights to enhance functionality and design.
