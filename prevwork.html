Ocean Blue Prime

Ocean Blue Prime is a premium steakhouse. Recently established, the client requested menus, outdoor signage, business cards, etc. that fits the vision of their business.

Using the provided logo and color palette I created a modern yet luxurious taste that reflected the products and their prices.

Since this was a newly established restaurant at the time, OBP needed branding, marketing, as well as meand of communicating with their clients. Outdoor signage adorned outside the restaurant to give it a presence in the area while also engaging and bringing in new customers. Business cards, and gift certificates weere created to further market the business to people in the neighborhood, giving them a reason to dine at the restaurant. The menus are used within the establishment and provide a stellar means to communicate between the business and its customers. Its elegant style relates to those of a higher expectation, allowing for the aura the business was looking to embody.
